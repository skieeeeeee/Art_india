In-House Manufacturing Process

We maintain strict quality control at every stage of production within our own facilities.

1. Material Sourcing

Selecting only premium grade yarn and raw materials for optimal strength and dye absorption.

2. Custom Dyeing

In-house color matching and dyeing facilities ensuring consistent, colorfast results.

3. Weaving & Tufting

Utilizing our 160 active looms to craft precise, durable patterns with expert artisans.

4. Quality Dispatch

Rigorous dust-free inspection, clipping, and secure packing for international shipping.
