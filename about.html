About Art India Tex

A legacy of premium textile woven manufacturing spanning four decades.

Company Roots

Crafting Global Textile Excellence Since 1980

Established in 1980, Art India Tex has organically grown from a passionate weaving endeavor into a leading global manufacturer and exporter of exclusive handmade rugs, carpets, and premium home textiles.

Located in Uttar Pradesh—the historic epicenter of Indian hand-woven craftsmanship—we merge generations of artisan skills with modern, precise, and ethical manufacturing standards to deliver export-grade quality to international clients.

  • In-house specialized weaving units ensuring unmatched quality control
  • Rigid child-labor free manufacturing environments
  • State-of-the-art custom dyeing and tufting capabilities
  • Streamlined global dispatch logistics for Europe and the Americas
